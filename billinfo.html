Bill Title

Summary

This draft bill proposes the establishment of the Information and Communications Technology Authority (ICTA) in Kenya. The ICTA will be responsible for developing and managing national ICT infrastructure, promoting digital literacy, and setting standards for ICT goods and services. The bill also outlines provisions for accrediting ICT service providers and ensuring compliance with ICT codes by public and private entities.

This bill proposes a new policy to enhance the public transportation system by increasing funding and improving infrastructure.

Background Information

Key Points

  • Increase funding for public transportation projects
  • Improve infrastructure and facilities
  • Introduce new eco-friendly buses and trains
  • Enhance accessibility for disabled individuals
